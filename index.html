Gallivanter🗺️

What started out as just going where the wind (read: my bank account) took me, developed into me wanting to fill all 20 gaps in the country tracker table I drew in my bullet journal. Somewhere between country 5 and 20, the travel bug hit hard and transformed a gap year activity into my ultimate (and most expensive) hobby.
